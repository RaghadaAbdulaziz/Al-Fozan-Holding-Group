The company

Al Fozan Holding Group

A leading Saudi conglomerate managing a portfolio of companies across multiple business sectors.

Who we are

Al Fozan Holding Group was established in 1959 and has grown into one of the largest family-owned conglomerates in Saudi Arabia, with more than 30 subsidiaries and joint ventures.

Where we operate

Headquartered in Al Khobar, Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia. Operations span the Kingdom and extend across the GCC and the wider Middle East region.

Our sectors

Retail, Real Estate, Industrial, Trading, and other diversified sectors — each managed by a dedicated subsidiary contributing to Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030.

Our history

Founded in 1959 by Abdullatif and Mohammed Al Fozan under the name Abdullatif and Mohammed Al Fozan. Over 60 years of steady growth, the group has become one of the Kingdom's most respected conglomerates.

Our people

Led by a talented and experienced team, Al Fozan Holding empowers its subsidiaries through governance, business development programs, and a commitment to sustainable growth.

Vision 2030

Al Fozan Holding aligns its business strategy with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, contributing to economic diversification, private sector growth, and community development across the Kingdom.
